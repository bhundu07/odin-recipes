Sadza Recipe

How To Cook Sadza

When you want to cook sadza you have to put all your ingredients in order.

The ingredients that you need to cook sadza are: water and mealie-meal.

Ingredients

  • water
  • mealie-meal

Steps

  1. Put 750 ml of water in a medium sized pot.
  2. Bring water to the boil.
  3. Put a small amount of mealie-meal into the pot and stir continuously until i thickens.
  4. Let the porridge splutter for a few minutes and then add the remaining mealie-meal a bit a t a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. When it thickens enough, let it simmer for 3 minutes and then serve with relish of your choice.
